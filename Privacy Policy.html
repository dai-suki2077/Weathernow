💻3. What Information We Collect

Weathernow is designed with your privacy in mind. We do not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) from our users.

3.1. Personal Information

Weathernow does not collect personal information such as your name, address, phone number, or email address. Location information is solely used to provide accurate weather forecasts. No PII is attached to the location data sent to third-party APIs.

3.2. Non-Personal Information

Non-personal information includes data such as your Android ID, device model, memory size, and usage patterns. This information helps improve our offerings and resolve issues.

3.3. Third-Party APIs

Weathernow relies on third-party APIs such as OpenWeatherMap and Open-Meteo for weather data. These APIs do not collect your personal information.

3.4. Location Approximation

If users do not provide their location, we may use the ipinfo.io/json API to approximate location based on IP address to deliver relevant weather data.

🔑4. Permissions

  • Location: To display weather forecasts for your current location.
  • Internet: To access weather data and basic content like background songs.
  • FOREGROUND SERVICE: For background updates and media playback.
  • Access Network State: To check internet connectivity.

👨🏫5. Children's Privacy

Weathernow is not designed for individuals under the age of 13. If we become aware of personal information collected from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly.
